Cabbage rolls

Description

Cabbage rolls are a classic Eastern European dish made from tender cabbage leaves filled with a savory mixture of ground meat and rice. They are simmered in a rich tomato sauce until perfectly cooked, making for a comforting and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of cabbage
  • 1 pound ground beef (or a mixture of beef and pork)
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Cabbage: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Carefully remove the core from the cabbage and place the head into the boiling water. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the leaves are soft and pliable. Remove from water and set aside to cool.
  2. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, cooked rice, chopped onion, minced garlic, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
  3. Assemble the Rolls: Gently separate the cabbage leaves. Place a small amount of the meat mixture onto each leaf, fold in the sides, and roll up tightly.
  4. Cook the Golubtsy: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Place the cabbage rolls seam-side down in the pot. Pour the tomato sauce and diced tomatoes over the rolls. Add sugar to the sauce if desired. Cover and simmer for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until the rolls are fully cooked.
